About

Old Town Menyala was a video mapping show and competition that was part of Jakarta Light Festival 2024. Contestants were tasked in creating a motion graphic animation that would then be projected onto the Fatahilah Museum.

Project tags

Motion graphic Design Illustration

Illustrations

Title Logo

The video mapping my team created focused on Jakarta throughout the ages, hence the title "Tenunan Waktu". In order to captue this, the logo type is designed to flow swiftly, signifying the flow of time. Accompanied with batik patterns from Batavia of flower designs, signifying growth, all wrapped and connected together by a red thread.



Assets Design

In this project, I mainly focused on creating the assets for the buildings and batik patterns. The colors of the illustrations itself represents the passing of time, with the use of primary colors at the start and the gradual appearance of more colors as the animation progresses.
