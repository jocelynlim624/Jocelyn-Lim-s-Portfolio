Implora

As part of my Visual Communication Advertising class, I had an assignment to rebrand an existing brand and make a competitor’s analysis on its marketing strategy. This project was in collaboration of my 3D Design project.

About

Implora is a cosmetics brand from Indonesia founded in 2022. Aside from make up products, Implora also produces an array of facial serums. Unfortunately, their facial serums are lacking exposure in the skincare industry. Thus, through this assignment, I did a rebranding focusing on their face serum line and did extensive research on possible marketing strategies to be used to increase brand awareness.

Case Study

Implora face serums adopts the minimalist style. Due to this, it makes it hard for consumers to distinguishing between products. Moreover, they wanted to market the 24k gold serum as the premium face serum in the series. But this attempt falls flat as it is marketed the same way as the other serums of the series. This combined with it’s flat colored packaging makes it seem out of place. This drawback is reflected on its sales, it ended up not being the top selling product in the series. Lastly, as Implora is initially a cosmetic brand, their skincare products lack brand awareness compared to their makeup line.



To promote the face serum series, Implora relies on advertising on social media platforms and its own websites. Their pop-up booths mainly focus only on their makeup products and lack a concise flow.

Through this project, I aimed to increase brand awareness on Implora’s face serum series by redesigning its packaging, advertising, booth design, as well as research marketing strategies based on competitors analysis.

Competitors Analysis

Looking at other serum series in the skincare industry, many differentiates its products either with different full flat colors or different shaped packagings. Other brands also succeed in presenting itself as not just a product, but as a movement of social empowerment. This heavily helps them build a good reputation among consumers and helps build a connection with its target market.

Strategy: Simple, Yet Clear







Finding your Audience

Through research and marketing surveys, I found that 41% of consumers of local skincare brands ranges from ages 21 to 30 years old. Based on their occupation, 44% of them were students. With Implora’s established presence on social media platforms, combined with it’s majorly young target audience, building an online community around its brand could expand consumer’s trust. Creating a roulette-styled social media filter surrounding its face serums could potentially pave ways to new audiences.

Booth Design

Vintage theater/ carnival ticket booth concept with vibrant colors. A better flow for visitors, more spacious with a photo spot at the back of the booth.
