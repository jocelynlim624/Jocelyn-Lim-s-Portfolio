Brand Identity

FETO has always had a similar logo each year, which is a peacock. For the logo I designed, it has a burst of gradients to represent the innovation and futuristic aspect of FETO 2024's theme.

Social Media Designs

In order to increase public awareness of the event, FETO posted frequently on its Instagram account (@feto.su). The designs of FETO 2024 took a more modern and chromatic feel with a burst of color. Other designs include posters and banners. Here are some of the visuals I designed.

Merch Designs

The merchandise sold were mainly themed surrounding the different majors in the Faculty of Engineering and Technology of Sampoerna University, featuring the faculty's mascot that I designed. The merchandise I designed ranged from stickers, pins, to keychains.
