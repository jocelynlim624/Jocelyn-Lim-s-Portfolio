About

Bangsa Gangsa is a research project supported by Sampoerna University aimed to preserve the culture of Gamelan Gangsa, a traditional instrument in Indonesia. The book and boardgames created through this research project are aimed to students, from the elementary to the senior high school level.

Book Illustrations

Boardgame design

The boardgames I created brings the players on a journey of creating their own Gamelan Gangsa. The first game features a monopoly style gameplay, where the players go around the board, competing to collect cards that contains the steps of creating their Gamelan Gangsa.


The second boardgames also takes the players along the vast steps of the creation if the Gamelan Gangsa. The board game is a vertical snakes and ladders, where players could either move forward or backwards based on the box with the “command card” the player lands on.
