About

Ramanika Cabinet was the Student Executive Board of the Faculty of Engineering and Technology Sampoerna University in 2023/2024. The cabinet has a vision of maximizing the use of technology and digitalization to integrate and empower the Faculty of Engineering and Technology, with the tagline “Where success meets purpose”.

Brand Identity

In order to establish its presence among the campus community, Ramanika Cabinet needed a strong branding to help carry out its purpose, both offline and on social media. With that, I aimed to create a visual language that could foster the visions of the cabinet, build a sense of community, and create a design system that could carry the cabinet forward. Ramanika Cabinet itself focused on purpose and needed a strong identity that represents its vision.




Meet Rumie!

As part of creating brand awareness amongst the residents of campus, creating a versatile and memorable mascot becomes crucial as it is the symbol of the cabinet. Rumie is a relatable university student, as well as a hummingbird, the symbol of Ramanika Cabinet.

Social Media Designs and Contents

Ramanika Cabinet ofter posts its contents and announcements on its social media. The contents range from informative posts, lighthearted comics and videos surrounding university life, and announcements of its upcoming events.
