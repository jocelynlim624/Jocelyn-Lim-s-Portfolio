Maxim Revamp

As part of my UI/UX Intensive Bootcamp final project from MySkill, we were assigned to revamp an application through extensive research and case studies in teams. My team chose to revamp the online transportation app called “Maxim”.

About

Maxim is an online taxi application that offers a variety of services, from deliveries to transportation. In this project, our team revamped Maxim’s application through stages of UI/UX research, design to user testing. Aside from research, as the creative director, I was mainly in charge of making the UI design and overseeing all creative choices.

Project tags

UI Design UI/UX Research

Case Study

Maxim has a lot of competitors in the industry, many of which with more structured application interface. Maxim is known for its simple, straight-to-the-point interface, however this system is not popular with users in Indonesia. The application also lacks several features that are present in others, such as the ability to send picture to the drivers to ease pick up, and rating the user’s experience. Our team aimed to further improve Maxim’s application interface to give a better experiences for its users and help Maxim stand out amongst its competitors.
